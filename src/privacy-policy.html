Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 14, 2026

Snap Print ("we", "us") respects your privacy. This page explains what information we collect and how we use it.

Information We Collect

When you place an order, we collect:

  • Name, email address, phone number, and shipping address
  • Order details (items, quantity, amount paid)

We do not collect or store your payment card details — payments are handled directly by Razorpay, our payment processor.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information only to:

  • Process and fulfill your order
  • Send order confirmations and shipping updates
  • Respond to your queries

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

Third-Party Services

We use the following trusted services to run our store, which may process your data as part of their service:

  • Razorpay — payment processing
  • Resend — transactional emails (order confirmations, notifications)
  • Cloudflare — website hosting and security

Data Retention

We retain order information for as long as needed to fulfill orders, handle disputes, and meet legal/tax record-keeping requirements.

Your Rights

You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you, or request its deletion (subject to legal record-keeping requirements), by emailing queries@snaprint.in.

Cookies

This site does not use tracking or advertising cookies. Only essential cookies required for checkout functionality are used.

Children's Privacy

Snap Print is not intended for use by children under 18 without parental supervision.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact Us

For any privacy questions or data requests, email queries@snaprint.in.
