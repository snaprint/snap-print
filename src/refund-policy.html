Refund & Return Policy

Last updated: July 14, 2026

At Snap Print, every item is made to order or printed in small batches, so please read this policy carefully before placing an order.

Damaged or Defective Items

If your order arrives damaged or defective:

  • Email us at queries@snaprint.in within 48 hours of delivery.
  • Include clear photos of the damaged item and packaging.
  • We'll offer a free reprint or a full refund, your choice, once the damage is confirmed.

Change of Mind

Because most items are custom-made or made to order, we're unable to accept returns or offer refunds for change of mind once production has started.

Custom / Made-to-Order Parts

Custom engineering parts and made-to-order items are built specifically for you and are non-refundable except in cases of defect or damage as described above.

Cancellations

  • Orders can be cancelled for a full refund only before production begins. Email us as soon as possible if you need to cancel.
  • Once printing has started, cancellation is no longer possible.

Refund Timeline

Approved refunds are processed back to your original payment method within 5–7 business days.

How to Reach Us

For any order issue, email queries@snaprint.in with your order details.
